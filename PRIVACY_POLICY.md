# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2024

## 1. Introduction

Prompt Store ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- We do not collect personal information
- No account registration required
- No login or authentication needed

### Usage Data
- Prompt usage counts (stored locally)
- Favorites list (stored locally)
- App preferences like dark mode settings (stored locally)

### Data Storage
- All data is stored locally on your device
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- No analytics or tracking services

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide you with the app's core functionality
- Store your created prompts locally
- Remember your preferences (theme settings)
- Track which prompts you've used (usage count)

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties because:
- All data stays on your device
- We don't use any analytics services
- We don't have any advertising partners

## 5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device using local storage mechanisms provided by your operating system.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's feedback channel.

---

**Note:** This is a local-only app. All your prompts, favorites, and settings are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any server.
